Your Language Partner

The Dr. Stelzhammer Language School was founded out of a passion for the German and English languages. Our founder, Dr. Stelzhammer, is an experienced language teacher who focuses on helping her students achieve their goals.

With a focus on high quality and affordable prices, we offer intensive courses, business German courses, and online language lessons. We pride ourselves on providing our students with the support and dedication they need to successfully learn German.
